I'm a designer, turned startup founder, now a product manager.

I like creating things—essays, websites, scripts, apps and startups. Anything which is positive sum, and impactful. Born in India. Currently living in London. Found a home on the internet. Writing is thinking. It's something I deeply enjoy. 10 years from now, or a 100, It might probably outlive me. This is an attempt at that posterity.

I'm currently working with [Springer Nature](https://www.springernature.com/gp) building solutions towards open science — open research, open data, open code, open everything.

I've been a [**3x bootstrapped startup founder** in the past](https://www.linkedin.com/in/shreyasprakash/) and come with 8+ years of experience building a wide range of digital products (SaaS/Edtech/Healthcare/Academic Publishing)—[chatbots for high-risk mothers](https://www.figma.com/proto/oi0zGtR5aAsiAJXsvhdcIA/RES_Noora-Health?page-id=0%3A1&type=design&node-id=1-2&viewport=566%2C571%2C0.12&t=lLUUl0X4c3vBratR-1&scaling=contain&starting-point-node-id=1%3A2&mode=design), [note-taking apps](https://www.claritynotes.io/), [smartphone apps for healthcare workers](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.carecompanionbd&hl=en&gl=US) and [IoT medical devices for hospital safety](https://krishnarajagopal20.wixsite.com/website-3), to name a few.
