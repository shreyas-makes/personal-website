# ANALYSIS



* Open Access journals see most delays because of the APCs that Authors need to find funding for
* Hybrid journals less than 2 days
* March and April 2024 has seen 1\.5 to 2 days average waiting for the OASiS stage to complete
* Scientific Reports is currently at 4\.1 days waiting at OASiS stage
* Low impact on Nature journals due to a bespoke author chasing tool being used \(developed by Group Production \- Stephen Russell\)
  * Incorrect email addresses
  * Author wants a non\-standard licence / waiver
