Build by SkipTheLine is a full-fledged ecosystem for anyone to go from being an employee to a builder to an entrepreneur. 

As a Founding team member, I helped more than 500+ builders to reach a combined $20,000 in revenue through their side projects. Build was able to grow in revenue by 4x ($100K+ in revenue) through various monetization strategies by launching various initiatives (Buildcamps, Build Cohort, Build newsletter, Build on Chain, Build Weekend, Build with AI)  

I led the programming of the Build cohort 4 — planning, hosting, designing events across the cohort, design and launch of new experimental offerings.  

Working closely with the founder to own, develop and execute the CRO plan based on insights and business objectives. Helped achieve 8% conversion rate and $15K+ in revenue through landing page optimizations, A/B Testing as well as newsletter subscriptions.  

As a designer-in-practise, I helped create quality UX wireframes and UI designs using Figma/Framer, collaborating with front-end development to ensure high-quality execution. Was also involved in conducting stakeholder validation and testing sessions using Figma prototypes and MVPs to ensure that products were fit-for-purpose and met user's expectations.
